About Us

Technovate is the burst of enthusiasm and young energy in the form of IIIT NR’s techno-cultural fest. The third edition of central India’s fest Technovate, witnessed thousands of visitors and participants from all over the country Days in Technovate is packed with leisure of entertainment and zeal of upcoming technical ideas. It provides an excellent opportunity to brush up your skills or find new talents to invest upon and obviously find the lost energy of youth back on the right spots of responsibilities and fun.

Technovate is the right place to meet new people and bond with them in the colourful shades of friendships which will make you finally end up with lifelong memories.

This year Technovate will again find it’s warm breath in your soon to be revered ideas and active participation. At the end you will be left thinking, “Let the wait be small and the meeting be long next time!”
